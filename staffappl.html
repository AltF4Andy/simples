Staff Application

  • What is your Minecraft username?

  • How old are you?

  • Have you ever been a staff member on a Minecraft server before? If yes, please provide details about your role, responsibilities, and duration of service.

  • Why do you want to become a staff member on our Minecraft server?

  • What qualities and skills do you possess that make you a good fit for a staff position?

  • How would you handle conflict or difficult situations between players on the server?

  • Are you familiar with the rules and guidelines of SimpleCraft? Please briefly describe them or provide a link to our server rules.

  • How much time can you dedicate to moderating the server each week? Do you have any time constraints or limitations?

  • Have you ever received any disciplinary actions or bans on any Minecraft servers? If yes, please explain the circumstances.

  • Do you have experience with any plugins or server management tools? If yes, please specify which ones.

  • Are you comfortable using voice communication (e.g., Discord) to coordinate with other staff members?

  • How would you contribute to the growth and improvement of our community?

  • Are you willing to adhere to and enforce the server rules impartially and without bias?

  • Is there anything else you would like to add to support your staff application?
