In-Game Rules for SimpleCraft

1. Respect and Be Kind:

Treat all players, staff, and the server with respect. Use appropriate language and avoid any form of harassment, discrimination, or bullying. Be helpful and considerate towards others.

2. No Griefing:

Do not destroy or modify other players' builds without permission. Respect their hard work and creativity. Pranks should be light-hearted and non-destructive.

3. No Cheating or Hacking:

Any form of cheating, hacking, exploiting glitches, or using unfair mods or client modifications is strictly prohibited. Play fair and maintain the integrity of the game.

4. No Spamming or Advertising:

Avoid excessive or repetitive messaging in chat, including advertising other servers. Respect the server's communication channels and use them responsibly.

5. No Stealing:

Do not take items, resources, or blocks from other players without their consent. Private chests, builds, and properties should be respected. Always ask for permission before entering someone else's area.

6. Obey Staff:

Follow the instructions and guidance provided by server staff members. They are here to maintain order, resolve conflicts, and ensure a positive gaming environment.

7. No Offensive Content:

Do not share or display any offensive, explicit, or inappropriate content, including language, skins, builds, signs, or usernames. Keep the server suitable for players of all ages.

8. No PvP without Consent:

Engaging in Player versus Player combat without the consent of all involved parties is not allowed. Respect other players' preferences and agreements.

9. No Lag Machines or Server Disruption:

Creating or using lag machines, excessive redstone contraptions, or any method to disrupt server performance is prohibited. Help maintain a smooth and enjoyable gameplay experience for all.

10. Report Rule Violations:

If you witness or experience any rule violations, report them to the server staff using the designated channels. Do not attempt to handle conflicts or enforce rules on your own.


Remember, these rules are designed to foster a friendly and fair gaming environment for everyone. Failure to comply may result in warnings, temporary bans, or permanent bans, depending on the severity of the violation. Enjoy your time on the server and have fun playing Minecraft!

Discord Rules for SimpleCraft

1. Be Respectful::

Treat all members with respect and kindness. Use appropriate language, avoid offensive content, and refrain from harassing, insulting, or bullying others.

2. No Spamming:

Avoid excessive messaging, repetitive posts, or flooding channels with unrelated content. Keep the chat organized and relevant to the respective channels.

3. No Advertising:

Do not promote or advertise other servers, communities, or unrelated products without permission. Respect the server's focus and avoid self-promotion.

4. Use Appropriate Channels:

Post content in the appropriate channels according to their designated topics. Keep discussions on-topic and avoid derailing conversations.

5. No NSFW Content:

Do not share or discuss explicit, adult, or not safe for work (NSFW) content. Keep the server suitable for users of all ages.

6. No Personal Information:

Refrain from sharing personal information, including addresses, phone numbers, or other private details. Protect your privacy and the privacy of others.

7. Respect Staff Decisions:

Follow the instructions and guidance of server staff members. If you have any concerns or questions, address them respectfully through the appropriate channels or direct messages.

8. No Unauthorized Bots or Scripts:

Do not use unauthorized bots, scripts, or automation tools without permission from server administrators. Maintain fair and equal participation for all members.

9. No Spoilers:

Be mindful of others' experiences and avoid sharing spoilers for games, movies, books, or other forms of media without proper warning or consent.

10. Report Rule Violations:

If you notice any rule violations, harassment, or inappropriate behavior, report it to the server staff through the designated channels or direct messages. Help maintain a positive and safe community environment.


Failure to adhere to these rules may result in warnings, temporary or permanent bans, or other appropriate actions determined by the server staff. Remember to enjoy your time in the Discord community and engage in respectful and meaningful conversations with fellow members.
